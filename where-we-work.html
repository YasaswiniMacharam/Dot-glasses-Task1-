Where We Work

Reaching the underserved. Changing lives globally.

Dot Glasses partners with local governments, NGOs, and community organizations across the globe to bring vision care to those who need it most. Our work is focused on underserved populations in remote, rural, and low-income areas.

Regions We Serve

  • Africa: Kenya, Ghana, Nigeria, and surrounding countries where access to eye care is extremely limited.
  • Asia: India, Nepal, and rural Southeast Asia where vision problems affect education and livelihoods.
  • Latin America: Regions in Mexico, Peru, and Bolivia in collaboration with health outreach initiatives.

Our Global Impact

With our scalable model, we have impacted the lives of tens of thousands of individuals. Each new location we serve brings us closer to a world where everyone has access to basic vision care.
